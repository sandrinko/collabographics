Collabographics

I design how people and AI think together.

Everyone has AI now. Almost nobody thinks with it well — alone, and even less so as a team. The tools arrived; the interfaces for actually thinking together haven't.

That's what I design and test. Studio courses where design teams ideate with AI in the loop on live Miro canvases. Shopping agents that style outfits with you, not for you — research now cited across the virtual try-on literature. And a series of working probes, built with Claude, that prototype what conversation should look like when one of the minds in it is artificial.

I've studied how groups think through interfaces since 2006 — brainstorming systems, meeting satisfaction, augmented shopping. AI didn't change my question. It added a new mind to the room.

I call this Collabographics.
